Why we’re needed

Emergencies are time-sensitive

Trauma, bleeding, breathing distress, toxin ingestion—minutes matter for stabilization and transport.

Owners aren’t equipped

Most people lack training, oxygen, safe restraint, and the right vehicle setup for urgent canine transport.

After-hours friction

Ballard clinics have limited hours. Emergency hospitals are centralized and can be busy—arrival without coordination can delay care.

Less panic, better handoffs

We reduce the emotional and logistical burden—clear destination, fast ETA, and a structured handoff to the receiving team.
